Logische architectuur
How — how do we structure the functionality?<br/><br/>This abstraction level is focused on identifying the kinds of business, data, application, and technology components needed to achieve the services identified in the conceptual level. It is about identifying how an architecture can be organized and structured, in an implementation-independent fashion. There will potentially be several ways to group services into logical components, based on principles and other grouping criteria, providing different logical solution alternatives.<br/>
